Avishkar Innovation brings India's rich heritage to life through advanced 3D printing and composite marble statues of legendary figures.

About Us

At Avishkar Innovation, our mission is to seamlessly blend India's timeless heritage with cutting-edge 3D printing technology. We are dedicated to innovation, precision, and craftsmanship, creating statues that honor our cultural icons with unparalleled detail and durability.

Founded as an Indian startup, we pride ourselves on producing composite marble statues of figures like Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Maharana Pratap, serving temples, institutions, and individuals worldwide.

Our Technology

Our 3D printing process uses state-of-the-art printers to layer composite marble material, ensuring high accuracy and scalability. This innovative method allows for intricate details that traditional sculpting can't match.

Advantages include exceptional durability, faster production times, and cost-effectiveness, making premium statues accessible to all.
